XTREME NAVIGATOR

Safety and Commercial needs are both met using this software. Contrary to other AI Algorithms in the market, this software is shore-based support and updates sailing guidelines to Master of the Vessel every six hours. It does not add to the very high burden the ships-staff already carries.

Instead of Vessel sailing on fixed straight line routes, this AI software ensures the best use of local currents, wind conditions to reduce adverse conditions and fully interacts with Vessels to prevent torque-rich running / heavy running.

Extreme Weather event handling and mitigation is a strong point of this AI algorithm, and evasive economical actions are provided vessels much in advance of the event. The guidance is continually updated as the event unfolds.

In conjunction with XTREME TUNEUP, this provides complete fuel economy and Voyage Optimization solutions to the owners. Both the software being LINUX-based are fully compatible and complement each other.
